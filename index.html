About Us

Radiant Fitness / Yoga Studio

Radiant Fitness & Yoga Studio is Karaikal's most trusted health and wellness centre, nestled in the heart of Kottucherry, Puducherry. With a perfect 5-star rating from over 93 happy members, we offer a unique blend of high-energy gym workouts and deeply relaxing yoga sessions — all under one roof. Our state-of-the-art equipment, hygienic facilities, and passionate trainers make every visit an inspiring experience.

Whether you are a beginner taking your first step towards a healthier lifestyle or an experienced fitness enthusiast looking to push your limits, Radiant Fitness is the perfect place for you. Our professional and friendly trainers provide personalised guidance to help you achieve your goals. Open six days a week with both morning and evening batches, we make it easy to fit fitness into your busy schedule. Come join our growing family and discover the best version of yourself at Radiant Fitness & Yoga Studio.
